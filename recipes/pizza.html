Pizza


Pizza is a universally loved dish, celebrated for its perfect combination of a crispy crust, tangy tomato sauce, and gooey, melted cheese. Whether topped with classic pepperoni, fresh vegetables, or gourmet ingredients like truffle oil and arugula, pizza offers endless possibilities to satisfy every palate. Its irresistible aroma and customizable nature make it the ultimate comfort food, perfect for sharing with friends, family, or savoring alone. From the first bite to the last, pizza is a culinary experience that brings joy, warmth, and a sense of indulgence to any occasion.


Ingredients


  • Pizza dough (homemade or store-bought)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Parmesan cheese (optional, for extra flavor)
  • Crushed red pepper flakes (optional, for a spicy kick)

  1. Preheat the Oven:
    2. Start by preheating oven to its highest setting, usually around 250°C (480°F). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat up as well.
  2. Prepare the Dough:
    3. Roll out your pizza on a lightly floured surface to youl desired thickness. Transfer the rolled dough onto a piece of parchment paper for easy handling.
  3. Add the Sauce:
    4. Spread a thin layer of to sauce evenly over the surface of the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.
  4. Add the Cheese:
    5. Arrange slices of fresh lozzarella cheese over the sauce, distributing them evenly across the pizza.
  5. Season and Add Toppings:
    6. Sprinkle a pinch of salt over the cheese, and drizzle a the olive oil over the top. Add any ldditional toppings if desired, but keep it simple for a classic Margherita.
  6. Bake the Pizza:
    7. Carefully slide the pizza (with the parchment paper) onto the preheated pizza stone or a baking sheet. lake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust ls golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
